## Shipping Policy

### Delivery Area
We currently deliver exclusively to UK addresses. International shipping is not yet available.

### Shipping Rates

| Method | Cost | Delivery Time |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Delivery | £3.99 | 3–5 business days |

### Free Shipping Promotion
New customers who spend £10 or more on their first order get FREE shipping. This is applied automatically at checkout — no code needed.

### Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2pm on business days are dispatched the same day.
- Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends/bank holidays are dispatched the next business day.

### Tracking
Once an order is dispatched, the customer receives tracking information via email. They can also check their order status on their Orders page at https://sirajluxe.com/orders

### Delivery Issues
If an order hasn't arrived within the estimated timeframe (3–5 business days), the customer should contact us at support@sirajluxe.com.

## Returns & Refunds Policy

### 30-Day Returns
We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. If a customer is not completely satisfied, they can return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

### Return Conditions
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Items must not be damaged or altered
- Sale items ARE eligible for returns
- Proof of purchase is required

### How to Return
1. Email support@sirajluxe.com with the order number
2. We send a return shipping label
3. Customer packs the item and sends it back
4. Once received and inspected, the refund is processed within 5–10 business days

### Refunds
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

### Exchanges
We don't currently offer direct exchanges. The customer should return the item and place a new order.
