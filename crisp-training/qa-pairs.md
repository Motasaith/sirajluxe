## SHIPPING

**Q: Where do you deliver?**
A: We currently deliver to UK addresses only. International shipping is not yet available.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: No, we currently only ship within the United Kingdom. We hope to offer international shipping in the future.

**Q: How much does shipping cost?**
A: Standard shipping costs £3.99 and takes 3–5 business days. New customers get FREE shipping on their first order when they spend £10 or more!

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within the UK.

**Q: Do you offer express or next-day delivery?**
A: We currently only offer standard delivery (3–5 business days). Express or next-day options are not available at this time.

**Q: How do I get free shipping?**
A: If it's your first order and you spend £10 or more, you get free shipping automatically at checkout — no code needed!

**Q: Is there a free shipping code?**
A: No code is needed! Free shipping is applied automatically at checkout for new customers who spend £10 or more on their first order.

**Q: When will my order be dispatched?**
A: Orders placed before 2pm on business days are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends/bank holidays are dispatched the next business day.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive tracking information via email. You can also check your order status on your Orders page at https://sirajluxe.com/orders

**Q: My order hasn't arrived. What should I do?**
A: If your order hasn't arrived within 5 business days of dispatch, please email us at support@sirajluxe.com with your order number and we'll investigate.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, email us immediately at support@sirajluxe.com and we'll try to update the address. Once dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address.

## RETURNS & REFUNDS

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. If you're not satisfied, you can return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, as long as they're unused and in original packaging.

**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: Email us at support@sirajluxe.com with your order number. We'll send you a return shipping label. Pack the item in its original packaging and send it back. Your refund will be processed within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect it.

**Q: Can I return sale items?**
A: Yes! Sale items are eligible for returns under the same 30-day policy.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. The refund goes back to your original payment method.

**Q: Do you refund shipping costs?**
A: Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item sent, damaged product).

**Q: Can I exchange an item?**
A: We don't currently offer direct exchanges. Please return the item for a refund and place a new order for the item you'd like instead.

**Q: What condition must returned items be in?**
A: Items must be unused, undamaged, unaltered, and in their original packaging. You'll also need proof of purchase.

**Q: I received a damaged or wrong item. What do I do?**
A: We're sorry about that! Please email us at support@sirajluxe.com with your order number and a photo of the issue. We'll arrange a return and full refund, including shipping costs.

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Q: The website isn't loading properly.**
A: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then refresh the page. If the issue persists, try a different browser. If you still have problems, email us at support@sirajluxe.com.

**Q: I can't add items to my cart.**
A: Make sure the item is in stock (check if the "Add to Cart" button is active). If the issue persists, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.

**Q: My discount/free shipping isn't being applied.**
A: Free shipping on first orders requires a minimum spend of £10 and only applies to your first order. Make sure your basket total is £10 or more before checkout.

**Q: I didn't receive my order confirmation email.**
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, sign in to your account and check your orders at https://sirajluxe.com/orders. If you still can't find it, email support@sirajluxe.com.
